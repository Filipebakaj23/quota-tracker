Getting Started
Your step-by-step guide to the Everfield platform

Everfield Quota Platform
Track your team's sales targets and performance in one place. Enter quotas in any format (monthly, quarterly, or yearly) and the platform converts automatically. Three things to do to get started:
① Add sellers
② Set their quotas
③ Log actuals regularly
1
Add your sellers & quotas
Create one entry per seller per period
Click "Add Entry" in the top-right corner of any screen. A form will appear where you fill in:
Seller name — the person's full name
Role — e.g. Account Executive, BDR
Period — Monthly, Quarterly, or Yearly
Quota — the target amount in your currency
Achieved — what they actually sold (update later)
💡 Tip: Use the "Periods" button on a seller card to fill in all 12 months at once instead of one by one.

Frequently asked questions
Can other companies see my data?
No. Your data is completely private and visible only to your company and the Everfield admin team.
What if I entered the wrong quota?
Click on a seller, go to their Profile, and use the edit (pencil) icon on any entry to update it.
Do I need to enter data every month?
Not necessarily. You can enter a yearly or quarterly quota and the platform spreads it automatically. Just update "Achieved" when you have actuals.
Which currency should I use?
Your default currency is pre-set by Everfield. You can change it per entry using the currency selector in the entry form.
